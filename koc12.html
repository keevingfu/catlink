Catlink KOC Social Media Performance Dashboard

Total Views & Total Engagement Over Time

Insights & Recommendations:

The overall chart indicates that total views surged significantly during mid-to-late January, driven by several viral posts across platforms. However, total engagement remains lower relative to views. We recommend analyzing the factors behind the viral posts and integrating stronger calls-to-action to boost interactions.

TikTok Performance Overview

Insights & Recommendations:

TikTok performance shows that while many posts reach high view counts, engagement (likes, comments, shares) is not as high. Consider incorporating interactive elements and posting at optimal times to further boost engagement.

YouTube Shorts Performance Overview

Insights & Recommendations:

YouTube Shorts consistently generate high views. However, engagement is moderate. Enhancing end screens, adding clear calls-to-action in descriptions, and encouraging comments may help increase viewer interaction.

Instagram Reels Performance Overview

Insights & Recommendations:

Instagram Reels display a wide range of performance with many posts achieving modest views and very low engagement. Experimenting with creative storytelling, trending hashtags, and interactive content such as Q&As or contests is recommended.
