Catlink Audience Insights & Research Questions

Executive Summary

This dashboard presents audience insights and addresses specific research questions for the Catlink "SE Testing R2 Ad set" campaign (Feb 9-15, 2025). Due to the single ad set, direct audience segmentation is limited. The campaign drives website purchases, with Ad T4 showing superior performance. Key research questions highlight the need for multiple ad sets to gain actionable audience data.

Audience Insights (Summary)

  • Website-Driven Conversions: All purchases occurred on the website.
  • Single Ad Set Limitation: Direct audience comparisons are not possible.
  • Inferred Characteristics: Likely cat owners, tech-savvy, seeking convenience, website shoppers.

  • Q8: Correlation between amount spent and conversions? Not a straightforward correlation. T4 has the lowest spend and high conversions.
  • Q9: How did the edits indicated by 'Last Significant Edit' impact the ads' performance? Positive.
  • Q10: Which purchase channel contributes most? Website purchases (100%).
  • Q11: Which audience segment is most responsive? Cannot be answered definitively without multiple ad sets. This requires implementing multiple ad sets with varied targeting.
  • Q12: Demographics, interests, and behaviors of converting users? Cannot be answered directly from this data. Requires website analytics and/or multiple ad sets.
  • Q13: Performance differences by device type? Data not available in this report. Requires Facebook Ads Manager or website analytics.
  • Q14: What times and which day performs the best? Data not available in this report. Requires Facebook Ads Manager.

Recommendations

  • Implement Multiple Ad Sets: Create distinct ad sets for audience segmentation.
  • Prioritize Ad Creative Improvement: Address low Quality Rankings.
  • Analyze Website Analytics: Use Google Analytics for deeper insights.
  • Monitor Key Metrics: Track ROAS, Cost per Result, CTR, frequency.
  • Optimize Ad T4: Continue allocating budget, but increase gradually.
  • Investigate T4's Post-Click Experience: Replicate its success factors.
  • Track All Purchase Channels: Ensure complete tracking if applicable.
  • Run customer surveys (Optional).
