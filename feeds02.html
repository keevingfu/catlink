Executive Summary

This dashboard provides an overview of the Facebook Ad campaign performance for Catlink's "SE Testing R2 Ad set" during the period of February 9-15, 2025. The campaign's primary objective is to drive offsite purchases tracked via the Facebook pixel. Ad T4 demonstrates superior efficiency. Key Findings and Analysis

  • T4 is the Most Efficient Ad: Ad T4 significantly outperforms the others in ROAS (29.92) and Cost per Result ($8.51).
  • ROAS Variability: Significant differences in ROAS across ads suggest creative/targeting effectiveness disparities.
  • Low Quality Rankings: All ads have "Below average" Quality Rankings, indicating a crucial improvement area.
  • Moderate CTR and CPC: Values are reasonable, but low conversion rates suggest post-click issues.

Recommendations

  • Prioritize Ad T4: Allocate more budget to Ad T4.
  • Investigate and Improve Ad Quality: This is critical. Focus on stronger headlines, ad copy, high-quality visuals, and A/B testing.
  • Review Landing Page Experience: Ensure consistency with the ad and optimize for conversions.
  • Monitor Frequency: Watch for ad fatigue, especially for T3 and T4.
  • Gradually Increase Budget for T4: Increase budget cautiously while monitoring performance.
