Catlink Ad Creative & Targeting Effectiveness

Executive Summary

This dashboard analyzes the creative and targeting effectiveness of Catlink's "SE Testing R2 Ad set" Facebook Ad campaign (Feb 9-15, 2025). The focus is on identifying strengths and weaknesses in ad creative and audience targeting. Key findings indicate a critical need to improve ad creative quality across all variations, despite average engagement and conversion rankings.

Key Findings and Analysis

  • Universally Low Quality Rankings: All ads received a "Below average" Quality Ranking, a major concern indicating poor ad creative relevance.
  • Average Engagement/Conversion Rankings: Targeting is potentially effective, but ads fail to fully capture attention and drive conversions efficiently.
  • CTR Variability: T1 has the highest CTR (1.96%), while T4 has the lowest (1.27%).
  • CPC Differences: T2 and T4 have higher CPCs ($1.31) than the others, possibly due to the low Quality Ranking.
  • Ad T4 Paradox: Lower CTR and higher CPC, *but* significantly better conversion rate, higher ROAS, and lower Cost per Result. This suggests better targeting and *much* better post-click experience for T4.

Recommendations

  • Prioritize Ad Creative Overhaul: Address the low Quality Rankings with clear messaging, strong visuals, compelling CTAs, and extensive A/B testing.
  • Investigate T4's Post-Click Experience: Analyze T4's landing page and checkout to replicate its high conversion rate.
  • Refine Targeting (Potentially): After creative improvements, explore more specific targeting.
  • Consider Audience Size and Frequency: Monitor reach for T4, it is converting best, but reaching the least users.
