Insights: These metrics provide a high-level overview of campaign performance. Tracking these over time reveals trends in engagement and sales conversion. Low engagement or conversion rates might indicate issues with content, targeting, or the product itself.
Recommendations:
  • Set benchmarks for each metric based on industry averages and past campaign performance.
  • Identify underperforming videos and analyze their content, posting time, and targeting to determine why they are not resonating with the audience.
  • Experiment with different content formats and styles to improve engagement.
  • If sales conversion is low, examine the entire sales funnel (from video view to purchase) to identify bottlenecks.
  • Consider A/B testing different calls to action (CTAs) in the videos.

Insights: This chart allows for a comparison of performance across different platforms (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok). It helps identify which platforms are most effective for reaching the target audience and driving engagement/sales.
Recommendations:
  • Allocate marketing resources based on platform performance. If TikTok is significantly outperforming other platforms, focus more effort there.
  • Adapt content strategies for each platform. What works on YouTube may not work on TikTok, and vice versa.
  • If a platform is consistently underperforming, re-evaluate the strategy for that platform or consider discontinuing efforts there.
