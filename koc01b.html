Insights:

1. Content from the brand's official account receives high engagement.

2. The engagement rate of sponsored content is generally lower than that of original content.

3. The audience is mainly concentrated in the 25-40 age group.

Recommendations:

1. Improve the creativity and authenticity of sponsored content.

2. Strengthen emotional connection with users in the target age group.

3. Encourage the production of more high-quality UGC content.

Organic vs Sponsored Engagement Rate

Insights:

1. Organic content generally has a higher engagement rate than advertising content.

2. Lifestyle content has the best advertising effect.

Recommendations:

1. Improve the naturalness of advertising content.

2. Optimize the placement strategy for different types of content.

Comments Trend Analysis

Insights:

1. Official account comment interaction is the most active.

2. The number of comments is highly correlated with content quality.

Recommendations:

1. Strengthen comment section operations.

2. Improve content quality to promote discussion.
